Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, profile photo (if provided).
  • Messages: Text, images, videos, audio files, and documents you send or receive.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, crash logs, and analytics data.

2. How We Collect Data

  • Account creation forms and profile updates.
  • Messages and media you send through the app.
  • Cookies and similar technologies for analytics and authentication.
  • Automatic collection via device and app interactions.

3. Why We Collect Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide messaging and social features.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To show relevant ads and measure their effectiveness.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Who We Share Data With

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database, authentication, storage), Firebase (authentication, storage, analytics), AppLovin (ads), Google (analytics, ads).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Other Users: Only the information you choose to share (e.g., messages, profile info).

4a. User-Generated Content and Moderation

  • You are responsible for the content you share in the app, including messages, images, and other media.
  • We reserve the right to moderate, remove, or restrict content that violates our terms or applicable laws.
  • Prohibited content includes but is not limited to: hate speech, harassment, illegal activities, and explicit material.

5. Data Deletion Requests

  • You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at samevibe197@gmail.com.
  • We will process your request within 30 days, subject to legal requirements.

5a. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to authenticate users, remember preferences, and analyze usage.
  • Third-party analytics and advertising partners may also set cookies or use tracking pixels.
  • You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect app functionality.

6a. Data Security and Retention

  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls.
  • Your data is stored securely on Supabase and other service providers.
  • We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

7a. International Users and Your Rights

  • If you are located in the European Union or other regions with data protection laws, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • To exercise these rights, contact us at samevibe197@gmail.com.
  • We process your data in accordance with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where relevant.

8a. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the app or email.
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact
